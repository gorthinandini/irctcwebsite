Explore Our Services

Flights

IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) extends its services beyond railways by offering domestic and international flight bookings at competitive prices. Through IRCTC Air, users can seamlessly book flights, avail exclusive offers, and enjoy a hassle-free travel experience.

Hotels

In the IRCTC ecosystem, the IRCTC Hotels service allows users to book accommodations across various cities in India. This service is integrated into the IRCTC website and mobile app, enabling travelers to book hotels along with their train tickets.

Tourism

The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) offers a variety of tourism services, making travel across India convenient and affordable. IRCTC Tourism provides options ranging from luxury train journeys to budget-friendly tour packages.

Car Rentals

IRCTC Car Rentals is a service provided by IRCTC in collaboration with major transport aggregators to offer convenient and affordable taxi services for travelers. Whether you're commuting within a city, heading to the railway station, or exploring tourist destinations, IRCTC ensures a smooth travel experience.

Rail your Packages

IRCTC offers Exclusive Rail tour packages with confirmed train tickets, sight-seeing and meals for enchanting Nilgiri Mountains, Darjeeling, Kullu Manali, Kashmir, Gangtok or divine tours of Mata Vaishno Devi, Holiday packages/ Land packages to these destinations are also available.
