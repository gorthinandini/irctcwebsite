About IRCTC

The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is a public sector enterprise under the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. Established to enhance railway passenger services, we provide seamless online ticket booking, catering, and tourism services across India.

Our mission is to deliver world-class travel experiences with convenience, safety, and innovation at the core. From the iconic Rajdhani Express to scenic tourist trains like the Palace on Wheels, we connect millions of passengers to their destinations every day.

With a focus on technology and customer satisfaction, IRCTC continues to revolutionize rail travel in India.
